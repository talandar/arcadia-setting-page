Etos, the god of excess and self-expression is also known as the god of hedonism and ambition.  They are worshipped by those who care about themselves more than others - pirates, brigands, thieves, and the like.  Many barkeeps make a visit to a shrine of Etos from time to time, to ensure that their clientele keep drinking. Clerics of Etos are pleasant, if loud and somewhat overbearing people. Some advice: never gamble with a cleric of Etos - even if they don't cheat, you'll probably lose.

### Divine Symbols & Sigils
Coin

### Tenets of Faith
- Gather Power and Accumulate Wealth
- Take the Advantage by any Means Necessary

### Holy Days
Etos' day is the second day of the week, Coin Day. This falls on the 2nd, 8th, 14th, 20th, and 26th of each month.
