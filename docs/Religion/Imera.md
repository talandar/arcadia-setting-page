Imera is credited with teaching people how to use fire, thereby bringing them together to form communities and eventually, civilization itself. Itinerant Clerics of Imera, called Brightseekers are often brought in to mediate disputes or arguments between people, as they are charged with bringing people together and forging bonds of community and togetherness. They also serve as judges or wardens in many areas. Even followers of other gods tend to accept rulings made by followers of Imera, as they are charged with upholding justice over much else.

### Divine Symbols & Sigils

Flame

### Tenets of Faith

- Forge Bonds of Togetherness
- Lift Others up to Lift up Everyone

### Holy Days

Imera's day is the fifth day of the week, Flames Day. This falls on the 5th, 11th, 17th, 23rd and 29th of each month.
