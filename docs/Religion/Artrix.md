Artrix is the god of knowledge, magic, and learning. They are commonly followed by tinkerers, scholars, and wizards. Clerics of Artrix are charged with making new discoveries, learning all they can, and sending back that information to the head church in Celestia. Both Artrix and their clerics are known for being inquisitive, almost to the point of being overbearing.

### Divine Symbols & Sigils
Open Book

### Tenets of Faith
- Seek out Knowledge Wherever Possible
- Create new Magic and Technology

### Holy Days
Artrix's day is the fourth day of the week, Tomes Day. This falls on the 4th, 10th, 16th, 22nd and 28th of each month.
