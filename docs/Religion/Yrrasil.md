Yrrasil is the god of nature, healing, life, and medicine. They also represent societal progress - the progress of people coming together to help each other. Usually Yrrasil is known for having itinerant clerics who provide healing and magical help to those in need. As a nature god, Yrrasil is also followed by many druids, rangers, and farmers.

### Divine Symbols & Sigils
Sprouting Plant

### Tenets of Faith
- Heal the Sick
- Grow Yourself and Those Around You

### Holy Days
Yrrasil's day is the third day of the week, Sprout Day. This falls on the 3rd, 9th, 15th, 21st and 27th of each month.
